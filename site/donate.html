FAQ

Support Without Confusion

The support page should stay explicit about what a donation is and what it is not.

Does a donation grant production rights?

No. Donations and GitHub sponsorships support development, but production usage still requires a separate commercial license under BSL 1.1.

Why use GitHub Sponsors?

It is already live for this account, familiar for developer audiences, and avoids adding a second payment stack to maintain right now.

Can a company both sponsor and license Wardex?

Yes. Sponsorship can support public development, while licensing covers production deployment and commercial use terms.

What kind of support matters most?

Recurring sponsorship is the strongest signal for roadmap sustainability. One-time donations still help with releases, docs, and maintenance spikes.
