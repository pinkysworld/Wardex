How AGPL 3.0 + commercial dual-license works in practice

Release version: v1.0.5

Open-source use (AGPL 3.0): Free for any use where you comply with the AGPL 3.0 network-service copyleft clause — including publishing your modifications if you run Wardex as a hosted service.

Commercial license: Required when AGPL copyleft compliance is not acceptable — e.g. proprietary products, hosted services without source disclosure, or when you need contractual SLAs and indemnification. Contact support@wardex.dev or see LICENSE.COMMERCIAL.

Verification: Release archives and container images ship with SLSA provenance, CycloneDX SBOMs and cosign signatures — steps in Reproducibility & Supply-Chain Verification.

Questions? Licensing, commercial use, or partnerships — contact us directly.

Licensing FAQ

Answers to the common questions.

Do I need to pay to try Wardex?

No. The Community tier is free for up to 10 endpoints and covers evaluation, research, homelab and non-production use. Paid tiers are only required when you run Wardex against live business operations.

What exactly counts as "production" use?

Any environment where Wardex is monitoring or protecting real business operations, customer data or live workloads. If a SOC depends on it to detect real attacks — that's production.

Is the product the same across tiers?

Yes. Every tier ships the same binary with all detection engines, SSO/SAML, RBAC, audit logs, HA reference guidance, air-gapped distribution, the full admin console, the complete API, and YARA/Sigma rule packs. Tiers differ on endpoint count, support SLA, and commercial support model — not on feature flags.

Do you see my telemetry?

Never. Wardex is 100% self-hosted. Agent events, alerts and analytics stay in your infrastructure. We don't operate a cloud backplane — there is nothing to phone home to.

Is Wardex truly open-source?

Yes. Wardex is licensed under AGPL-3.0, an OSI-approved open-source license. The full source is readable, forkable, and modifiable. A commercial license is available for organisations that cannot comply with AGPL's network-service copyleft requirement.

Can I switch tiers or cancel?

Yes. Monthly plans cancel at any time — you keep the right to run the version you licensed for the remainder of the period. Annual plans renew yearly. No lock-in — the source stays yours.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes. Academic, non-profit and early-stage startup discounts are available. Annual prepay is ~17% off monthly. Email us with context and we'll reply within 48h.

What happens after the Change Date?

With AGPL-3.0 dual-licensing, the source is already fully open-source from day one. There is no Change Date. Commercial licenses are perpetual per version and allow use without AGPL copyleft obligations.
